Account Recovery with a One-Time Link

If you forget your password, you can securely recover access to your Journey account without contacting support. Journey uses an one-time token (OTT) sent to your registered email address. Your password is never sent by email.

info

The recovery link is private and intended only for you. Do not forward it or share it with anyone. For your security, the link is temporary and can be used only once.

Request a recovery link

  1. Open the Login page.
  2. Select Forgot Password? below the password field.
  3. Enter the email address registered with your Journey account.
  4. Select Send Recovery Link.
  5. Check your inbox for the recovery email. If it is not there, check your spam or junk folder.

Sign in with the recovery link

  1. Open the recovery link from the email on the device where you want to use Journey.
  2. Journey automatically verifies the one-time token and signs you in.
  3. After successful verification, you are redirected to the Home page.
schedule

Having trouble with the link?

A recovery link may have expired, already been used, or been copied incorrectly. Return to the Account Recovery page and request a new link. If you did not request the email, you can safely ignore it.
