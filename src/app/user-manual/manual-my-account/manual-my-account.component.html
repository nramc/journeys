My Account

The My Account section allows you to manage your account settings and preferences.

  • My Account is accessible in the top tool bar with name My Account.
  • It provides a user-friendly interface for updating your profile information.
  • You can change your password.
  • You can view and manage your account security settings.
  • You can add/remove Passkeys.
  • You can activate/deactivate Multi-Factor authentication.
  • You can activate/deactivate Time based One Time Token (TOTP).
  • You can view your account activity and login history.
  • You can delete your account.
