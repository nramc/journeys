Introduction

Journey is a online platform that allows you to create, manage, and share your travel experiences. Whether you're planning a trip, documenting your adventures, or reminiscing about past journeys, Journey provides the tools you need to capture and share your memories.

With Journey, you can:

  • Create personalized travel itineraries
  • Document your experiences with photos, videos, geographical information and narration
  • Share your journeys with friends and family
  • Access your journeys from anywhere, anytime
  • Track your travel history and statistics
  • Visualize your Journeys in map and calendar views
  • Relive in your past lovable memories with notifications
